YOUR PRIVACY
IS NOT FOR SALE

We built Pulse with a single principle: your data belongs to you. Not advertisers. Not governments. Not us.

🔐

Signal Protocol E2E Encryption

Every message, call and file is encrypted end-to-end. Only you and the recipient can read your messages — not even Pulse can.

🛡

Zero Knowledge Architecture

Our servers only see encrypted blobs. We store the minimum data needed to operate — and none of your message content.

🔑

Two-Factor Authentication

Protect your account with biometrics, PIN, and optional 2FA. Unauthorized access is blocked at every layer.

🧹

Disappearing Messages

Set messages to auto-delete after 24 hours, 7 days, or any custom interval. Your past doesn't have to follow you.
